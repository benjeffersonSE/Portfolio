Work Experience

Capgemini

During Year 10 I was also very lucky to be selected from a number of students to complete 2 weeks work experience at Capgemini in Telford.

Whilst there I was given an insight on how a large organisation uses I.T. and what it is like working in an office environment.

This included shadowing IT technicians and understanding how they used Oracle SQL databases for HMRC taxes.

Telford Wrekin Council

Completed 36 hours of work experiance at the Telford Wrekin Council

Shadowed Web Developers and learned how they developed interactive websites for local schools

This included languages such as HTML, CSS/Bootstrap and JavaScript/JQuery. This furher developed my skills working in a professional environment within an IT sector

Job Centre

An additional 36 hours of work experience at the Job Centre in Telford.

Helped less technically experienced users apply for universal credit from the government website.

This taught me important skills such as patients and communication while assisting users.

Primark

As an employee I was responsible for stocking, clearing and recovering the Home department.

Being the only member of the department, this helped deal with task management and problem solving independently.

Later I was moved to another department where I worked within a group of 3 to 4 members of staff.

This further improved my communication and teamwork skills something which I found helpful and essential when working on tills and assisting customers.

This has also improved my problem-solving skills and patience as often we will have an unhappy customer, or a customer with a problem such as an inquiry about stock. I use these skills to try and resolve a customer issue to the greatest outcome possible.

Working at Primark introduced me into a professional environment and taught me a wide variety of vital skills such as teamwork, time management and customer service. These are incredibly important skills which are transferable and suited to a workplace.
